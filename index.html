How We Can Help

Octave is a modern studio built for the needs of busy, working professionals.

We know that finding the right therapist or coach can be hard. Our clinicians are experts in their field, and train other clinicians at places like Columbia, Mt. Sinai, and NYU. Collectively, we can handle a broad range of situations and will pair you with the right partner for your needs. We have built proprietary, custom tools to ensure that you are getting results from your time with us.

Now offering therapy, coaching, and classes.

Therapy

In-person sessions with an empathetic clinician to help you identify old patterns and nurture new growth. We are experts in a wide variety of evidence-based therapies.

Coaching

One-on-one program offered virtually, focusing on targeted skills from cognitive behavioral therapy and mindfulness to help you move past challenges and towards your goals. This can be an effective, less expensive alternative to therapy, or can serve to supplement therapy.

Classes

Our classes cover a range of topics, from improving sleep to mindfulness techniques, helping you incorporate emotional resilience into your everyday life. Drop into a class to learn and unwind, or join weekly to build a practice and community.
