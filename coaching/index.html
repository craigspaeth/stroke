Why Coaching?

Octave’s coaching program combines goal setting and skill building in a personalized, data driven approach that helps you achieve tangible results in your life.

You will work with a coach to co-create a plan that supports the achievement of your goals via setting weekly action steps, accountability and the application of evidenced-based tools.

Independent research has shown that learning psychology skills can drive a 50% improvement in mood, providing a compelling alternative way to look after your well-being.
